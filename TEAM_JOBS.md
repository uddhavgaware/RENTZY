# RentXY Non-Coding Business Roles & Professional Guidelines

Since you are handling all the engineering and coding with AI, you can assign these crucial business and operational roles to your friends. A successful startup is 50% code and 50% operations!

---

## 🕵️ 1. Quality Assurance (QA) & Beta Tester
**The Goal:** Break the app before real users do and ensure a flawless user experience.

**Tasks to Assign:**
* **Cross-Device Testing:** Test the platform on iPhones, Androids, laptops, and tablets.
* **Edge Case Testing:** Try clicking things too fast, submitting empty forms, or uploading massive files.

> [!TIP]
> **How to do QA professionally:**
> 1. **Never say "It doesn't work".** Always provide a structured Bug Report.
> 2. **Professional Bug Report Format:**
>    * **Issue:** (e.g., App crashes when uploading a profile picture).
>    * **Steps to Reproduce:** 1. Go to Profile. 2. Click Upload. 3. Select a 10MB image. 
>    * **Expected Result:** It should show a "File too large" error.
>    * **Actual Result:** The screen turns white and freezes.
>    * **Device Info:** iPhone 13, Safari browser.
> 3. **Take Screenshots/Screen Recordings:** A 5-second screen recording is worth a thousand words for the developer (you!).

---

## 📝 2. Content & Data Entry Manager
**The Goal:** Make the app look alive, trustworthy, and heavily populated so new users aren't looking at a ghost town.

**Tasks to Assign:**
* **Seed Data:** Go out and find real (or highly realistic) properties around your college. Upload 10-20 Flats and PGs.
* **Mover Testing:** Create dummy accounts and generate fake moving requests.

> [!TIP]
> **How to do Data Entry professionally:**
> 1. **Quality over Quantity:** A professional platform has high-resolution, well-lit photos. Do not upload blurry images or pictures with watermarks from other websites.
> 2. **Professional Copywriting:** 
>    * **Bad:** "nice flat for rent near college."
>    * **Professional:** "Spacious 2BHK Flat Near Campus | Fully Furnished | 24/7 Security & Water."
> 3. **Attention to Detail:** Double-check prices, deposit amounts, and map pins before clicking submit. Bad data destroys trust in a new startup.

---

## 🛡️ 3. Admin & Operations Moderator
**The Goal:** Keep the platform safe, secure, and free of scammers.

**Tasks to Assign:**
* **KYC Verification:** Log in as `ADMIN` and manually review Aadhar/ID cards and selfies.
* **Listing Moderation:** Check every newly posted property to ensure it complies with your guidelines.

> [!CAUTION]
> **How to moderate professionally:**
> 1. **Zero Bias:** Verify documents strictly according to the rules. The name on the ID *must* match the name on the account exactly. 
> 2. **Security First:** Never share user data, phone numbers, or ID photos with anyone outside the team. Data privacy is a legal requirement.
> 3. **Spam Detection:** Look out for "too good to be true" listings (e.g., a luxury penthouse for ₹2,000/month). These are usually scammers trying to steal deposits. Reject them immediately.

---

## 📣 4. Growth & User Feedback (Marketing)
**The Goal:** Get the first 100 real, active users and gather raw feedback.

**Tasks to Assign:**
* **User Interviews:** Watch 5 college students use the app for the first time.
* **Social Media Hype:** Post screenshots and teasers on college WhatsApp groups or Instagram.

> [!TIP]
> **How to gather feedback professionally:**
> 1. **Do not guide the user.** When conducting a user interview, give them a task (e.g., "Try to book a mover"). Sit silently and watch where they get stuck. Do not tell them what to click!
> 2. **Ask open-ended questions.** Instead of asking "Do you like the design?", ask "What was the most confusing part of this page?"
> 3. **Categorize Feedback:** Don't just give the developer a messy list of complaints. Group feedback into themes (e.g., "3 users didn't understand how Split Expenses worked", "2 users couldn't find the logout button").
