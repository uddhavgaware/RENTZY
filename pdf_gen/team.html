RentXY Ecosystem

The people who make zero-brokerage renting possible

Property Owners

Flat & Villa Listings

Owners looking to rent out their vacant properties quickly without paying middlemen.

Key Job Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Property Management: Create detailed listings, upload high-quality photos, and set competitive rent prices.
  • Tenant Screening: Review potential tenants, verify backgrounds, and ensure KYC compliance.
  • Property Maintenance: Ensure the property is clean, safe, and move-in ready for new tenants.
  • Rent Collection: Manage digital rent agreements and track monthly rent payments.

Hostel & PG Managers

Shared Accommodations

Administrators managing multiple rooms and beds for students and working professionals.

Key Job Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Inventory Management: Track live bed availability, occupancy rates, and room assignments.
  • Facility Operations: Oversee daily food operations, WiFi, laundry, and housekeeping services.
  • Resident Support: Address resident complaints, enforce hostel rules, and ensure a safe environment.
  • Bulk Administration: Handle digital onboarding and monthly invoicing for dozens of students at once.

Packers & Movers

Relocation Partners

Verified logistics vendors that help users securely move their furniture and belongings.

Key Job Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Estimation & Quoting: Assess moving requirements and provide transparent, upfront pricing.
  • Packing Services: Safely pack fragile items, dismantle furniture, and secure belongings for transit.
  • Logistics & Transport: Safely load, drive, and unload goods from the source to the destination.
  • Customer Service: Maintain punctuality, resolve damages (if any), and ensure a stress-free move.

Tenants & Roommates

End Users

Students, families, and professionals looking for their next home or a compatible roommate.

Key Job Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Property Hunting: Search, filter, and shortlist properties based on budget and lifestyle preferences.
  • Financial Planning: Budget for security deposits, monthly rent, and moving expenses.
  • Property Care: Maintain the flat or room in good condition and adhere to the owner's or hostel's rules.
  • Roommate Matching: Communicate with potential roommates to ensure compatibility in dietary and lifestyle habits.
